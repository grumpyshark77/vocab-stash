Getting Started

Three steps. That's it.

1

Highlight a word

Select any word or phrase on a webpage while you're reading.

2

Right-click → Add to Vocab Stash

A small form appears. Type your translation, add notes and tags, and hit save.

3

Review anytime

Click the Vocab Stash icon in your toolbar to browse, search, and export your growing word list.
